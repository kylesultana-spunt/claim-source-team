Independent verdicts on what politicians say.

We track public claims made by Maltese politicians and parties, check them against primary sources like NSO, Eurostat, Budget documents and parliamentary records, and publish the evidence. No spin, no allegiances.

spunt.mt — a non-partisan fact-checking project covering the 2026 Maltese general election. Verdicts are produced with AI-assisted research, cross-checked against primary sources, and reviewed editorially where confidence is low.
